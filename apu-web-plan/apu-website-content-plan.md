## 2) Page-by-Page Content Plan (What Must Exist)

## A. Homepage (Must Exist)

**Goal:** one-page institutional overview + conversion to apply.

Must-have blocks:

1. **Institution positioning statement** (who APU is, what it stands for)
2. **Featured stories/news** (3–6 items)
3. **Admissions quick actions**
   - Apply now
   - Admission guide
   - Academic calendar
4. **Study programs snapshot** (7 programs, link each)
5. **Scholarships snapshot** (types + deadline window)
6. **Research highlights** (4 centers, short intros)
7. **Message from leadership** (chair/president excerpt)
8. **Student systems quick links** (SIAKAD, LMS, E-library, exam)
9. **Contact + address + WhatsApp + social links**
10. **Key facts strip** (program count, research centers, scholarship types, etc.)

## B. Admissions Page (Must Exist)

**Goal:** end-to-end applicant journey.

Must-have blocks:

1. **Who should apply / intake year**
2. **Registration waves + deadlines**
3. **Application pathways** (online + on-campus)
4. **Application fee + payment method**
5. **Step-by-step admission process** (submission → test → interview → offer → payment → verification)
6. **Assessment schedule & result timeline**
7. **SPI payment rules**
8. **Refund policy**
9. **Required documents checklist**
10. **Apply CTAs** (portal, direct registration, guide PDF, camaba login)
11. **Admissions contact channels**

## C. Scholarships Page (Must Exist)

**Goal:** scholarship discovery + eligibility clarity.

Must-have blocks:

1. **Scholarship period + intake year**
2. **Common timeline (register → H+7 announcement → validation/NIM)**
3. **Scholarship catalog**
   - Academic
   - Non-academic achievement
   - Content creator
   - (4th type: clear naming/definition)
4. **Benefit tables by scholarship type**
5. **Requirements per type**
6. **How to apply**
7. **FAQ (stacking? max benefit? docs format?)**
8. **Scholarship contact/help desk**

1. **Academic philosophy statement**
2. **All study programs (7) with links**
3. **Program structure levels** (Bachelor, Diploma, Profession)
4. **Academic calendar**
5. **Learning platforms (LMS/SIAKAD)**
6. **Library + exam systems**
7. **Academic regulations / handbook links**
8. **Pathways after admission** (orientation, advising, class readiness)

## 4) Critical Content Gaps to Fill Before Writing Pages

| Priority | Scope | Why now | Target pages/outputs |
|---|---|---|---|
| **P0 (Must launch)** | Content needed for applicant conversion + institutional trust baseline | Needed for first public release and admissions flow continuity | **Admissions**, **Scholarships**, **Academics (core profile)**, **About APU**, **Contact**, **Homepage (assembled from P0 pages)** |
| **P1 (Should follow right after launch)** | Content that strengthens credibility and student operations | Important for depth and operational clarity, but can follow once P0 is live | **Research (initial center + publication evidence layer)**, **Student Life & Services (service descriptions/SOP/contact matrix)**, **News & Stories (starter 12 items + editorial owner)** |
| **P2 (Can phase after stabilization)** | Expansion content requiring broader institutional data readiness | Depends on policy/process maturity or extended governance data | **Leadership (full roster/bios beyond chairman)**, **International (policy-grade operations: visa/onboarding/housing/partner status)**, **advanced KPI blocks** (research KPIs, graduate outcomes where not yet verified) |

Top priority missing data:

#### **Program hard data (all 7 programs) (P0)**
   - SKS total
   - Official duration (semester/year)
   - Accreditation status + certificate number + validity period
   - Language of instruction
   - Intake capacity/quota

#### **Program curriculum and outcomes evidence (P0)**
   - Semester-by-semester curriculum matrix
   - Program-level tuition/fee structure (if shown in Academics)
   - Career outcome evidence (employment/further study/sample employers)

#### **Scholarship taxonomy normalization (P0)**
   - Confirm final scholarship list and rules in one canonical source

#### **Institutional contact and channel consistency (P0)**
   - Official institutional email directory (general, admissions, scholarship, research, international)
   - Social media official handles
   - Standardized contact naming, response-hours policy, and helpdesk routing

#### **Research output evidence (center-level) (P1)**
   - Flagship project portfolio (title, objective, status, outcomes)
   - Publications normalization for APU (last 3 years, DOI/status, attribution, center mapping)
   - Patents/IP/HKI records
   - Research KPI baseline (papers, partnerships, impact indicators)

#### **Research governance and people (P1)**
   - Center head names/titles
   - PI/researcher directory per center
   - Ethics/compliance process (IRB/IACUC-equivalent if applicable)
   - Data integrity and grant governance contacts

#### **Student Life & Services operational content (P1)**
   - Student Affairs, Counseling/Wellbeing, Career, Academic Advising profiles
   - Access SOPs, required docs, service SLAs, escalation paths
   - PIC/contact set per service (email/WA/location/hours)
   - Complaint/grievance and safety escalation channels

#### **Student systems support layer (P1)**
   - One-line purpose text per system
   - Login/helpdesk contact per system
   - Issue-report workflow
   - Support hours / uptime notes (if available)

#### **News/Stories content pipeline (P1)**
   - Minimum 12 starter items across institutional, research, and student stories (can be seeded from SCCR + International news)
   - Events/announcements feed source and update owner
   - Editorial governance: owner, review rule, publication cadence, archive taxonomy

#### **Leadership scope expansion (optional, phase 2) (P2)**
   - Leadership roster + bios (if expanded beyond chairman-only approach)

#### **International operations detail (P2)**
   - International admissions policy (requirements, deadlines, fees, checklist)
   - Visa/immigration guidance and ownership
   - Inbound/outbound onboarding SOP (pre-arrival to first month)
   - Housing/living support (accommodation, transport, safety, cost-of-living)
   - Partner directory (scope, MoU status, active period)
   - International office SLA + escalation + emergency contact

#### **Policy and compliance publication set (P2)**
   - Student handbook and code of conduct
   - Academic integrity, anti-harassment, privacy/data policy
   - International policy set (transfer/credit, grievance, refund/cancellation)
