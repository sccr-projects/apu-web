## 5) Data Request Checklist (to complete a credibility-rich Research page)

Collect these fields per center:

### Center Identity
- Official center head name and title
- Short mission statement

### Outputs
- Last 3 years publication list
- Last 3 years conference presentations
- Patents/IP/HKI records
- Flagship projects with summary + status + outcomes

### People and Infrastructure
- Core researchers list
- Lab facilities and key instruments
- Safety/compliance standards

### Collaboration
- Active partners (university/industry/government)
- Open collaboration opportunities
- Contact person + email + form

### Governance
- Ethics and review process
- Data/research integrity policy
- Funding and grant governance
