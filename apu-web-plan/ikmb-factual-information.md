## Standard Admission Process (6 Steps)

### Step 1: Application Submission

**Pathways:**

- On-Campus Application: Admissions Office, APU Campus, Level 1
- Online Application Portal: <https://bit.ly/PMB_IKMB>

**Application Fee:**

- Non-refundable Application and Placement Assessment Fee: **IDR 500,000**
- Fee secures eligibility for institutional placement assessment
- Upon payment confirmation, candidates receive official assessment schedule

### Step 2: Academic Placement Assessment

- **Schedule:** Every Saturday, 09:00 WIB until completion
- **Formats:**
  - On-Campus Assessment at Agung Putra University, APU Campus
  - Online Assessment via Secure Zoom Proctored Session
- **Results:** Released on Monday following the assessment
- **Outcomes:**
  - Admission to Interview Stage
  - Non-Admission Decision

### Step 3: Academic Interview and Advisory Session

Conducted by Academic and Admissions Committee. Includes:

- Academic readiness evaluation
- Personal motivation assessment
- Program alignment consultation
- Financial and academic advisory session (with parents/guardians)

### Step 4: Admission Offer and Enrollment Confirmation

Successful candidates receive official Admission Offer.

**Institutional Development Contribution (SPI) Payment Structure:**

- 50% of total SPI payable within 7 days of admission confirmation
- Remaining 50% payable following national university admission announcement (SNBP/SNBT, approximately August)
- Enrollment officially secured upon completion of initial payment
- Admission is selective and competitive due to limited program capacity

### Step 5: Refund Policy

- Students admitted to Indonesian Public Universities (SNBP/SNBT) with official documentation: eligible for **30% refund** of SPI paid
- Students enrolling in other private institutions: **not eligible for refund**

### Step 6: Official Payment Channel

- **Bank:** Bank Rakyat Indonesia (BRI)
- **Account Name:** Institut Karya Mulia Bangsa
- **Account Number:** 0327-01-001770-56-3

### Step 7: Payment Verification

Submit payment confirmation including:

- Full Name
- Proof of Transfer

**Via Official Admissions Office WhatsApp:** +62 858-6426-8019

## 1. Academic Scholarship

### Description

- For high-achieving students with strong academic performance during high school
- Granted based on subject grades and overall academic results
- Reflects consistent excellence and academic readiness for university-level education
- Supports students demonstrating outstanding academic dedication
- Aims to encourage continued academic success at Agung Putra University

### Scholarship Benefits

- **Tuition fee reduction of up to 50% SPI**

### General Requirements

1. Graduated from SMA/SMK/Equivalent or currently in Grade 12
2. Minimum average score of selected major subjects: **85** (e.g., Mathematics, Biology, Sociology, depending on study program)
3. Minimum overall report card average: **80**
4. Submit academic transcripts/report cards
5. Submit online registration form
6. Complete and upload the official assessment form provided

---

## 2. Non-Academic Achievement Scholarship

### Description

- For prospective students with outstanding accomplishments beyond academic performance
- Recognizes achievements in: sports, arts, non-academic science competitions, technology & innovation, leadership, organizational involvement, officially recognized competitions
- **Special scheme for Tahfidz (Qur'an memorization)** as part of character strength and spiritual achievement
- Offers tuition fee (SPI) reductions based on achievement level within last 3 years
- Supports talented individuals in continuing higher education while developing their potential

### Eligible Achievement Categories

- Sports
- Arts (music, dance, theater, fine arts, etc.)
- Non-academic science competitions
- Technology & innovation
- Leadership / Organization
- Officially recognized competitions
- Tahfidz (Qur'an memorization)

### Achievement-Based Scholarship Benefits

| Level | Benefit |
|-------|---------|
| International | 100% SPI |
| National | 75% SPI |
| Provincial | 50% SPI |
| City/Regency | 30% SPI |
| District/School | 10% SPI |

**Maximum benefit:** Up to 50% SPI (as stated in poster)

### Tahfidz Scheme Benefits

| Juz Memorized | Benefit |
|---------------|---------|
| 30 Juz | 100% SPI (UKT Semester 1-2 reduction subject to interview evaluation) |
| 15 Juz | 50% SPI |
| 10 Juz | 35% SPI |
| < 10 Juz | 20% SPI |

### General Requirements

1. Graduated from SMA/SMK/Equivalent or currently in Grade 12
2. Achievement must be obtained within the last **3 years** (for non-academic categories)
3. Provide official certificate, award letter, or recognition from authorized institution
4. For Tahfidz applicants: provide official Tahfidz certification stating number of Juz memorized
5. Submit online registration form
6. Complete and upload the official assessment form provided
