A Word from the Dean
We do not merely teach law. We cultivate thinkers who shape the very fabric of justice in our society.

It is my distinct honour to welcome you to the Faculty of Law. For over six decades, we have stood at the forefront of legal education in this region, producing graduates who have gone on to serve as judges, policymakers, advocates for change, and architects of our legal landscape.

Our commitment extends beyond academic excellence. We foster a community where rigorous intellectual discourse meets compassion, where tradition informs innovation, and where every voice finds its place in the pursuit of justice.

Professor Eleanor Tan
Dean, Faculty of Law
